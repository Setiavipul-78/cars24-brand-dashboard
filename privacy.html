Privacy Policy — Cars24 Key Metrics

Application: Cars24 Key Metrics · Effective 15 July 2026

1. Overview

Cars24 Key Metrics ("the Application") is a private, internal marketing-analytics dashboard operated by CARS24 Services Private Limited ("Cars24", "we", "us"). It aggregates performance metrics for Cars24's own official social-media and search presences into a single view for the Cars24 marketing team. The Application is not offered to the public, does not have public sign-up, and is used solely by authorised Cars24 employees.

2. Information We Access

The Application reads analytics data only for accounts, pages and channels that Cars24 owns and that the authorising user administers, through the official APIs of each platform:

  • LinkedIn (Community Management API): organisation Page follower trends, page/visitor statistics, aggregated audience demographics, and the reach/engagement of content published by the Cars24 Page. We access these via the r_organization_social and rw_organization_admin permissions, which are granted only by an admin of the Cars24 LinkedIn Page.
  • YouTube (YouTube Data & Analytics API): channel-level views, watch time, subscribers, traffic sources and audience data for Cars24's own YouTube channels.
  • Instagram / Meta (Instagram Graph API): follower and content-performance metrics for Cars24's own Instagram business accounts.
  • Google Search Console & Google Sheets: branded-search and campaign data for Cars24's own web properties and internal tracking sheets.

We do not access the private profiles, personal messages, or individual member data of any LinkedIn member or other platform user beyond the aggregated, Page-level analytics the platform itself exposes to Page administrators.

3. How We Use the Information

Accessed data is used exclusively to display internal marketing dashboards and reports for the Cars24 team — for example, tracking follower growth, content performance and share-of-search over time. We do not use it for advertising targeting, and we do not make automated decisions about individuals.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share accessed data with any third party. Data remains within Cars24's internal dashboard environment and is visible only to authorised Cars24 personnel.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Aggregated metrics are stored within the Application's private data store for the purpose of showing historical trends. Access tokens are stored securely and are used only to refresh data on Cars24's behalf. Data is retained for as long as it is useful for internal trend analysis and is deleted on request.

6. Revoking Access

An authorising administrator can revoke the Application's access at any time from the relevant platform's settings — for LinkedIn, via Settings & Privacy → Permitted Services; for Google/YouTube, via Google Account Permissions. Revoking access immediately stops all further data retrieval.

7. Security

Access is limited to authorised Cars24 team members. Credentials and access tokens are kept in secured configuration and are never exposed in the dashboard or shared externally.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Application evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

For any questions about this policy or the data the Application accesses, contact the Cars24 marketing analytics team at vipul.setia@cars24.com.
